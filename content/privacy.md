**PRIVACY POLICY**

*I Think Not, Inc. (\"Envoy\")*

**Effective Date:** April 9, 2026

**1. Introduction**

I Think Not, Inc. (\"Company,\" \"we,\" \"us,\" or \"our\") operates the
Envoy platform (\"Service\"), an AI-powered customer support email
platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose,
and safeguard your information when you use our hosted Service at
byenvoy.com or any related websites, APIs, and applications. If you
self-host the open-source version of Envoy, this Privacy Policy applies
only to the extent you interact with our hosted infrastructure (for
example, authentication services or telemetry, if enabled).

By accessing or using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If
you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

**2. Information We Collect**

**2.1 Account Information**

When you register for the Service, we collect your name, email address,
company name, and billing information (processed by our third-party
payment provider).

**2.2 Customer Support Data**

The Service processes email content and customer support communications
that you or your end customers transmit through the platform. This data
is used to generate AI-powered responses and improve the accuracy of the
Service for your account.

**2.3 OAuth and Third-Party Integrations**

Envoy connects to third-party services via OAuth, including Gmail
(Google), Microsoft (Outlook/Exchange), and Shopify. When you authorize
these integrations, we receive access tokens and the data necessary to
read, send, and manage emails or retrieve product and order data on your
behalf. We do not store your passwords for these services.

**2.4 Usage and Analytics Data**

We automatically collect log data, IP addresses, browser type, device
identifiers, pages visited, feature usage, and performance metrics to
improve the Service and diagnose issues.

**2.5 Cookies and Tracking Technologies**

We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to maintain
sessions, remember preferences, and analyze usage. You can control
cookie settings through your browser, but disabling cookies may affect
Service functionality.

**3. How We Use Your Information**

We use the information we collect to:

-   Provide, operate, and maintain the Service, including generating
    AI-powered email responses.

-   Process transactions and manage your subscription.

-   Connect to and synchronize data with third-party services you
    authorize (Gmail, Microsoft, Shopify).

-   Improve, personalize, and expand the Service.

-   Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support.

-   Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and technical issues.

-   Comply with legal obligations.

**4. Data Retention**

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active
or as needed to provide the Service. Customer support data processed
through the Service is retained for the duration of your subscription
unless you request earlier deletion. Upon termination of your account,
we will delete or anonymize your data within 90 days, except where
retention is required by law.

**5. Data Sharing and Disclosure**

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

-   Service providers and subprocessors who assist in operating the
    Service (hosting, payment processing, analytics), subject to
    contractual data protection obligations.

-   Third-party integrations you authorize (Gmail, Microsoft, Shopify)
    to the extent necessary for functionality.

-   Law enforcement or government authorities when required by law,
    subpoena, or court order.

-   In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, in which
    case your data may be transferred to the successor entity.

**6. Data Security**

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to
protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest,
access controls, and regular security assessments. However, no method of
electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot
guarantee absolute security.

**7. International Data Transfers**

If you are located outside the United States, your information may be
transferred to and processed in the United States. We take appropriate
safeguards to ensure your data is protected in accordance with this
Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws, including Standard
Contractual Clauses where required.

**8. Your Rights**

**8.1 General Rights**

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access,
correct, delete, or port your personal data, as well as the right to
restrict or object to certain processing. To exercise these rights,
contact us at privacy@byenvoy.com.

**8.2 GDPR (European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland)**

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, our legal bases for
processing include: performance of a contract, legitimate interests,
consent, and legal obligation. You have the right to lodge a complaint
with your local data protection authority.

**8.3 CCPA (California)**

California residents have the right to know what personal information is
collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal
information. We do not sell personal information. To make a request,
contact privacy@byenvoy.com.

**9. Children\'s Privacy**

The Service is not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly
collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have
collected data from a child under 16, we will delete it promptly.

**10. Open-Source Self-Hosted Instances**

If you self-host Envoy using the open-source codebase, you are the data
controller for all data processed by your instance. This Privacy Policy
does not govern data processed solely on your infrastructure. However,
if your self-hosted instance communicates with our servers (for example,
for license verification or optional telemetry), this policy applies to
that data.

**11. Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you
of material changes by posting the new policy on our website and
updating the effective date. Your continued use of the Service after
changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
